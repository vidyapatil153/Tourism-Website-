Bamboo Garden

Bamboo Garden, situated in Amravati, Maharashtra, holds the distinction of being India’s first dedicated bamboo park. Spanning an impressive 49 acres, this eco-friendly tourist destination is a testament to the significance of bamboo in environmental conservation, sustainable living, and economic development. The park was established with the vision of promoting bamboo cultivation, conservation, and its vast applications in daily life. Designed to serve as an educational and recreational hub, Bamboo Garden attracts a diverse range of visitors, including nature enthusiasts, students, families, and researchers. It offers a unique opportunity to explore the ecological importance of bamboo, its role in carbon sequestration, and its potential as a sustainable alternative in construction, handicrafts, and industrial applications.

Timings: 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM

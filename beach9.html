Chivla Beach

Hardly a kilometer from the Malvan region, Chivla Beach is a divine site that offers much more than the beautiful views. It even promises to enthrall tourists with the adventure activities that are organized here. Nature admirers and couples are often seen thronging this place as it is clean, pristine and peaceful. Also, it is surrounded by the tourists on full moon nights for a nocturnal walk. The view of the moon popping out of wild sea looks amazing. Do visit the food joints close to the beach for to try some yummy sea food.

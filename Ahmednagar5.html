Randha Falls

Randha Falls is a powerful and scenic waterfall located near Bhandardara in Maharashtra’s Ahmednagar district and is formed by the Pravara River plunging into a deep gorge. The waterfall is especially spectacular during the monsoon season, when the water flow becomes intense and creates a dramatic and thunderous sight. Surrounded by rocky cliffs, lush greenery, and misty surroundings, Randha Falls offers breathtaking viewpoints that highlight its raw natural beauty. The area around the falls is ideal for nature photography and sightseeing, though visitors are advised to maintain safety due to strong currents. With its majestic flow, dramatic landscape, and refreshing atmosphere, Randha Falls is one of the most impressive natural attractions in Maharashtra.

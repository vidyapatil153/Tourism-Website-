Rehekuri Blackbuck Sanctuary

Located in the serene landscapes of Maharashtra’s Ahmednagar district lies the Rehekuri Blackbuck Sanctuary, a hidden gem for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. Established in 1980, this sanctuary spans approximately 2.17 square kilometers and is dedicated to the conservation of the elegant Blackbuck, also known locally as ‘Kalvit’. Despite its modest size, Rehekuri offers a unique opportunity to witness these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat. Located near the village of Rehekuri in Karjat Taluka, about 80 kilometers from Ahmednagar city, the sanctuary is characterized by its dry deciduous scrub forest and expansive grasslands

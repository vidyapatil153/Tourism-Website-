Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Museum

This museum in Aurangabad is dedicated to the exemplary life of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It provides a window to learn history in a relaxed manner and makes for a good site, especially for adolescent kids. The galleries of this edifice comprise pictures of forts he conquered or built during his lifetime. Parts of the museum exhibit weaponry and other war artifacts used during the campaign against the Mughals. It is located near Salim Ali Sarovar and opposite the Himayat Bagh or the Mughal Garden.

Timing : 10:00 am to 05:00 pm.
